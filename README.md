# hc!Chess

[![hc!Chess](https://img.shields.io/badge/hc!Chess-Chess%20App-blue)](https://himc29.github.io/hcOS/)

hc!Chess is a website for playing pass and play chess.

Check it out live: [hc!Chess Website](https://himc29.github.io/hcChess/)  

> ⚠️ **Early Release / In Development**  
> hc!Chess is still in active development. Some features may be missing or incomplete, and things may change in future updates.  

---

## Features

- **Move Validation** – Robust logic for all moves. (Castling, promotion, and en passant will be added soon)  
- **Game End Detection** – Detects when checkmated or stalemated. (Will be added in the future)  
- **Timer System** – Support for various time controls. (Will be added in the future)

---

## How to Play

Visit the website:  

[https://himc29.github.io/hcChess/](https://himc29.github.io/hcChess/)  

- Click the Play button to start playing.  
- Click on the piece to select and press on a square to move it there.  
- Pass and play with your friend.  

You don’t need to install anything—everything runs directly in your browser.
